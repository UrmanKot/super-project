ADVANTAGES

The advantage of R-WAVE ERP is full cycle of manufacturing process. R-WAVE ERP contains and supports all the processes necessary for the production of the final product, from the structure of the product, purchase, storage in a warehouse, and ending directly with production and subsequent sale

In R-WAVE ERP we use special concepts:
- product structure
- production list
- production plan
- procurement chain

These special concepts allow you to organize an end-to-end production process for the final product. The product structure defines the composition of the product, the sequence of assembly and required technologies.
